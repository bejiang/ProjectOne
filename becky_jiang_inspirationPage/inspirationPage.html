I am inspired by Beth Cavener's sculptures. I love the theme of exploring human psychology in her work through the physical medium of clay. There’s so much conveyed with the textures she incorporates into her pieces, and the scale of her art is amazing. Her sculptures inspire me to tackle larger projects as well.




I am inspired by Tina Yu’s art. I love how detailed and intricate each piece is, and how you notice more things the longer you look. I also like seeing how she incorporates Chinese culture into her designs. Her work inspires me to think about how I can incorporate my heritage into my art.





I like the music artist Glaive. I find his music to be comforting and think he’s a good storyteller. His work inspires me to think more critically about how to tell stories through my designs, and how to connect multiple pieces through a shared theme. I like working with his music on in the background.
