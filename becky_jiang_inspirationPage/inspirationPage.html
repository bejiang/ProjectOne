Beth Cavener

I am inspired by Beth Cavener’s sculptures. I love the theme of exploring human psychology in her work through the physical medium of clay.
There’s so much conveyed with the textures she incorporates into her pieces, and I also think the scale of her pieces is amazing.
Her larger-than-life sculptures inspire me to tackle larger projects as well.



Tina Yu

I am inspired by Tina Yu’s art. I love how detailed and intricate each piece is. I also like seeing how she incorporates Chinese culture into her designs.
Her work inspires me to think about how I can incorporate my heritage into my art.



Glaive

I like the music artist Glaive. I find his music to be comforting and think he’s a good storyteller.
His work inspires me to think more critically about how to tell stories through my designs.
